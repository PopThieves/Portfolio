My time at Northcoders

The Northcoders experience gave me an insight into all aspects of a full stack software developer. The fundamentals section of the course cemented my knowledge in JavaScript and introduced me to NodeJS, and some intermediate concepts such as closure, recursion, asynchronisity, and the importance of test driven development.
The back-end section presented the importance of servers and databases using HTTPS, ExpressJS, and SQL. The front-end section introduced the React framework which I became very comfortable using and enjoy it's responsive design.
The final project phase of the course presented an experience similar to that of employment in software development, team focused with agile project management.
Overall I was extremely satisfied with my time at Northcoders and the continued support that they offer after graduating, I met some amazing people and got to socialise during a time which caused so much isolation for people. They gave me the tools to start independent projects and create things that I am interested in and would personally use. Cannot recommend them enough if you are new to coding and software development.
